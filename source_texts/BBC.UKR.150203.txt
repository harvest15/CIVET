HEADLINE: Eight civilians said killed in shelling in past 24 hours in eastern Ukraine

Text:
Six civilians were killed, including an 11-year old girl, and 18 were injured in the past 24 hours in Donetsk Region as a result of military activity, the Ukrainian Interior Ministry has said. Most of the victims were from Debaltseve, where shelling by the terrorists killed five people. Another person from Avdiyivka was also killed. Law enforcement has opened criminal cases.

Also, the Interior Ministry said that people are being evacuated from population centres subject to militant shelling.

[Two civilians have been killed in Luhansk Region in the past 24 hours, the Interfax-Ukraine news agency reported at 0832 gmt on 3 February, quoting Luhansk state regional administration head Hennadiy Moskal as saying.]
